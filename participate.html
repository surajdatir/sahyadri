Participate in the Sahyadri Network

Join builders, researchers, and contributors shaping decentralized infrastructure.

Whether you run a node, write code, create content, or build infrastructure — there’s a place for you in the Sahyadri community. The network is open to everyone, and every contribution strengthens the ecosystem.
